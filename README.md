# 5indit Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: July 5, 2026**

5indit ("we," "us," or "our") operates a multi-service digital platform in Nigeria that enables users to connect with people, discover and engage vendors, book hotels and apartments, browse and list properties, and access related marketplace services. We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our users.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the 5indit mobile application, website, APIs, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By using 5indit, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

---

# 1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide, maintain, improve, and secure our Services.

## 1.1 Personal Information

### User Information

When you create an account or use our Services, we may collect:

* Full name
* Username and profile information
* Phone number
* Email address
* Profile photo (optional)
* Date of birth (where applicable)
* Gender (optional)
* Residential or business address
* Account login credentials
* Communication preferences

### Social & Community Information

To help users connect with others, we may collect:

* Friends, followers, and connections
* Messages and communications sent through the platform
* Posts, comments, reviews, ratings, and uploaded content
* Interests and preferences
* Event participation and invitations

### Vendor Information

If you register as a vendor, business, or service provider, we may collect:

* Business name
* Business registration information (where applicable)
* Business address
* Contact information
* Business category
* Product and service listings
* Business operating hours
* Payment and payout information

### Hotel & Apartment Booking Information

When booking accommodations, we may collect:

* Guest details
* Booking dates
* Number of guests
* Special requests
* Booking history
* Payment details necessary to complete reservations

### Property Listings

For property owners, agents, or managers, we may collect:

* Property descriptions
* Property images and videos
* Property location
* Pricing information
* Ownership or management details
* Contact information

### Payment Information

Payments made through 5indit are processed securely by trusted third-party payment providers. We do not store complete debit or credit card information on our servers.
